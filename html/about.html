CALABRESE CERAMICS

Anna Calabrese (*1989) studied Philosophy, Social Science and History of Science in Basel, Leipzig and Berlin. She is led by her interest in modes of handling knowledge - pottery is one of them.

Each peace is handbuilt, handglazed and therefore unique.

Ceramics available at @voostore and @the_good_store_berlin.
